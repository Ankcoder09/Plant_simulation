🌱 Why We Did This Experiment

This experiment demonstrates how genetic modification can improve a plant's resilience to varying sunlight levels. By simulating different light conditions, we observe how the GM plant maintains its growth compared to the normal plant.

🌞 Purpose

To show how genetically modified plants can sustain growth and productivity under environmental stress, especially in areas with inconsistent sunlight exposure.

💡 Conclusion

The GM plant adapts effectively, staying lush even under low sunlight and slightly wilting only under extreme light. This highlights biotechnology's role in sustainable agriculture and future food security.
